# Privacy Policy

**Study Companion**
**Last updated:** April 7, 2026

## Overview

Study Companion is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your study materials are processed entirely on your device, and the developer has no access to your data.

## Data You Create

When you use Study Companion, the app stores the following data that you create:

- Scanned or imported photos of study materials
- Text extracted from those photos via optical character recognition (OCR)
- AI-generated summaries and flashcards
- Your organizational structure (subjects, chapters, and topics)

All of this data is created by you and belongs to you.

## On-Device Processing

Study Companion performs all processing on your device:

- **Text recognition (OCR)** uses Apple's Vision framework, which runs entirely on-device. No images or text are sent to any server for processing.
- **AI features** (summaries and flashcards) use Apple Intelligence via the FoundationModels framework, which runs entirely on-device. No data is sent to any external AI service.

## iCloud Sync

If you are signed in to iCloud, Study Companion uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your study data across your devices. This sync uses your **private iCloud database**, which means:

- Only you can access your synced data.
- The developer cannot read, access, or retrieve your data.
- Sync is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure and is subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

You can disable iCloud sync for the app in your device's Settings under iCloud.

## Data Collection

Study Companion does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- No analytics or telemetry are collected.
- No advertising frameworks or ad tracking are used.
- No data is sent to third-party services.
- No account creation or sign-in is required (iCloud sign-in is managed by your device, not the app).

## Camera and Photo Library Access

Study Companion requests access to your camera (for document scanning) and photo library (for importing images). These permissions are used solely to capture or select study materials. Photos are processed on-device and are not transmitted anywhere.

## Data Storage and Deletion

Your data is stored locally on your device and, if iCloud sync is enabled, in your private iCloud database. You can delete any subject, chapter, or topic within the app at any time. Deleting the app removes all local data. To remove iCloud data, you can manage your iCloud storage through your device's Settings.

## Children's Privacy

Study Companion does not collect any personal information from any user, including children under 13. Since all processing is on-device and no data is transmitted to the developer or third parties, the app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
